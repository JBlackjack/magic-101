TRICK 1: FOUR TO THE TOP

Remove the four jacks from the deck and three different cards as
well. Hold the different cards face up in your hand, then lay the
four jacks spread out on top keeping the three different cards
concealed. Show the four jacks, then square all the cards and place
them face down on top of the deck. Tell the story of four robbers
robbing the bank. Slide the top card off the packand place it into
the deck near the bottom, the cellar. The next one goes in a little
higher, the first floor. The third jack above that, the second floor.
The last one stays on top, the roof. You have now slid what appears
to be three jacks into the deck, but in reality you only moved the
three different cards. Talk about the police arriving and the only
way to escape was through the roof. Turn the top four cards over
revealing the jacks.
